UnderRated Artists

Welcome to Underrated Artists, our mission is to put out artists that aren't very popular or never been heard of and show them the recognition they deserve.

Underrated Artists is about introducing new artists and new music to people. Providing the value of them and their music that they need. Nowadays people are into mumble rap and off beat nonsense which is whack and not real music at all.

Music is not the same anymore, there is not as much depth nor storytelling. Music labels are producing weak artists and making them popular which is why people are missing out on the real talented artists like Sylvan LaCue, Rayana Jay, SiR, Vince Staples, etc. Underrated Artists is a place where you can find what's missing in the music industry, and receive that depth and storytelling.

I want people to listen to these artists who don't receive the recognition for their music,their depth, their realness and themselves. I would like to recommend people to take a break from the popular music that doesn't make sense and give these artists a chance to hear them out.
